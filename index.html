Hajj & Umrah Training

Every year Akij Expeditions arranges training programs for the Hajj and Umrah performers in order to provide them the proper guideline and minimizes any unwanted incidents while traveling or started this Holy Journey.

The prospective pilgrims who engage in the Hajj and Umrah services of Akij Expeditions, have to undergo this training.

  • Have a better understanding of pilgrimage requirements
  • Understand the proper performance of compulsory and commendable religious rituals, as well as the forbidden practices during the pilgrimage

Why Akij Expeditions

Akij Expeditions is a concern of Akij group


Akij group is one of largest business conglomerates in Bangladesh. Akij has been operating in different industries with a sound reputation and trust for the last 60 years. In order to serve its best to the customers, the group stepped in the travel industry with a vision to become the most reliable company in the travel services by satisfying customer expectations.


In order to achieve the vision, the management of the company has been working relentlessly to develop system, policies, mechanism & quality human resources who can sincerely carry the image of the group.
